# 🛑 WHAT NOT TO SAY

**Avoid these pitfalls:**

1. ❌ Vague descriptions ("machine learning system")
2. ❌ Overconfident claims ("production-ready", "90% accurate")
3. ❌ Lack of understanding ("I'm not sure why X matters")
4. ❌ Context-free metrics ("90% accurate" — on what?)
5. ❌ Wrong priorities ("optimized for latency" — in medicine, accuracy first)
6. ❌ False claims ("small models are useless")
7. ❌ Sloppy technical language ("some embedding model from HuggingFace")
8. ❌ Dismissing limitations ("no problems found")

**Instead**: Use quantified trade-offs, acknowledge constraints, show awareness of medical stakes
